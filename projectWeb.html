The first modern car was invented by Karl Benz in Germany, it was powered by a gasoline engine, this was in the year one thousand and eight hundred and eighty-five, Karl Benz patented his patent on January 29, 1938, in Mannheim, Germany,
There was also Conniot car; Conniot car is a steam car that was invented by Nicholas Conniot in 1977, One of the first cars in history, Connieut was a French army engineer, and invented it for the purpose of dragging heavy equipment for the army, especially the guns. Later, in 1896, Henry Ford manufactured the first four-wheel gasoline-powered vehicle; the first air-pressure tires were manufactured in the previous year and were manufactured by Mechelen. Since then, the automotive industry has witnessed a very large development; many different designs have been made for cars.
